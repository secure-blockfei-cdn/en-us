FAQ

To log in to your BlockFi account:

  1. Visit the official BlockFi website at blockfi.com.
  2. Click on the "Login" button in the top-right corner.
  3. Enter your registered email address and password.
  4. Complete any required security steps, such as two-factor authentication (2FA).
  5. Click "Submit" to access your account.

If you forget your password:

  1. On the login page, click "Forgot Password?"
  2. Enter your registered email address and click "Send Reset Link."
  3. Check your email inbox for the password reset link.
  4. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password.
  5. Use the new password to log in.

If you're unable to log in, consider the following:

  • Double-check your email address and password for errors.
  • Ensure your internet connection is stable.
  • Verify that your two-factor authentication (2FA) code is correct.
  • If you suspect your account is locked or compromised, contact BlockFi support immediately via their official help center.
